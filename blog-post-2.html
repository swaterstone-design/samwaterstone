Snippets From My Year in Rwanda

September 10, 2022 | Shooting Touch

I still haven't quite wrapped my head around it all—leaving Rwanda, working at BWB, traveling, returning home—but at the moment, I just feel immense gratitude for the chance to explore the world, meet some of NBA's top coaches and executives, and to engage with the next generation of African basketball stars.

My year in Rwanda through the Shooting Touch fellowship was transformative. I arrived thinking I knew what I was coming to do, but I left having learned infinitely more than I could have imagined.

The people I met, from the women on the courts to the coaches and community leaders, showed me what resilience, determination, and joy look like. Basketball became the vehicle for so much more—for conversations about opportunity, for building relationships across difference, for creating spaces where young people could imagine futures for themselves.

I'm grateful to everyone who was part of this journey, and I can't wait to see where the work of Shooting Touch continues.
